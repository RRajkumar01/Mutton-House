About Mutton House

At Mutton House, we specialize in serving authentic, hygienic, and flavor-packed mutton dishes inspired by rich Bihari culinary traditions. Every recipe is crafted with love using premium ingredients, traditional spices, and slow-cooked techniques that bring out the deep, soulful taste of Bihar. Whether you're craving homestyle meals or festive delights, we’ve got you covered. Our kitchen is proudly FSSAI certified, ensuring the highest standards of food safety and hygiene. From daily cravings to party orders, Mutton House is your trusted destination for delicious, wholesome mutton — made with tradition, served with pride.
